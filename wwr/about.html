Wild River Rafting

Purpose: To provide unforgettable river adventure experiences that foster a connection with nature, teamwork, and personal growth, always upholding the highest standards of safety and sustainability.

Our History

Wild River Rafting was founded in 2015 by a group of outdoor enthusiasts who shared a deep passion for rivers, nature, and adventure. What started as a small operation with just one raft and a handful of local guides has grown into one of the most trusted rafting companies in the region.
